My Story

My name is Aditya and I love Monday mornings.

Computers have been my passion in life for many years now, and I never grow tired of learning about new technologies or figuring out novels ways to put existing technologies to use. A software developer, I have accrued two years of experience as a front-end web developer, learning real-world applications for the skills that I have been developing throughout my life.

Following the passion for web development and learning advanced technologies in Computer Science, I shifted from Mumbai to New York. I am currently earning my Master of Science in Computer Science at the State University of New York at Binghamton.

To me, my biggest short-term goal is to find a professional environment where I can make a positive impact and put my skills to good use.
